About Me

I am Gouri Sankar A, a dedicated Cyber Security Researcher, Bug Hunter, and Junior Software Developer based in Kottayam, Kerala. With a strong passion for cybersecurity, I have successfully identified and secured vulnerabilities on prominent platforms such as lsgkerala.gov, NIT Calicut, mahaconnect.in, gjust.ac.in, Victoria College, and several other government domains. My work in bug hunting has not only helped strengthen the security of these platforms but has also fueled my drive to contribute to a safer digital ecosystem.


I completed my schooling at De Paul Public School, Nazarathuhill, Kuravilangad, and pursued my higher secondary education at Sree Krishna Vocational Higher Secondary School, Kurichitanam. Currently, I am focused on self-learning and research to enhance my skills in bug hunting and the automation of vulnerability scanners. As a junior software developer, I combine my programming expertise with cybersecurity knowledge to build secure and efficient solutions. My journey is driven by a relentless curiosity and a commitment to continuous growth in the ever-evolving field of cybersecurity.
